What I Wish My Teacher Knew About Me 

Directions:
Write 5-7  complete sentences (Or more if you want!) answering the prompt below. Be honest and specific—your answers will help your teachers support you this year.

Prompt:

What is one thing you want your teacher to know about you—either as a person, a student, or both?
You can talk about how you learn best, things you struggle with, things that motivate you, or something important from your life that you want your teacher to keep in mind.

   Prompt Ideas

About Me as a Person

  • Something that makes me happy is… Gayming™ and fixing things outside.
  • A big goal I have for myself is… To get into ESports (I applied for a bachelor for ESports!)
  • One thing that makes me unique is… I’m a furry :3
  • Something important happening in my life right now is… Drivers Ed

About Me as a Student

  • I learn best when… I am hands on doing whatever the assignment is.
  • I get frustrated in class when… I get told “It’s easy” or “This is simple, why can’t you do it??”
  • I feel proud of my work when… I get a B or higher.
  • It helps me when my teacher… Lets me do the work myself until I request help myself.

Challenges & Needs

  • If I’m having a hard day, it helps when my teacher… Leaves me alone.
  • When I’m stuck, the best way a teacher can help is… Let me figure it out myself until I request help.
